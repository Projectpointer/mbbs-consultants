❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a free counselling session?
Fill the consultation form on this page (Free Counselling card), or simply WhatsApp us on the sticky WhatsApp button. We'll reach out within 2 hours.
What are the eligibility criteria for MBBS abroad?
Minimum 50% in PCB (40% for reserved), NEET qualification mandatory. IELTS/TOEFL not required for many countries.
What is the total cost including tuition & living?
Depends on country: Russia: 25–35 lakhs, Bangladesh: 35–45 lakhs, Philippines: 30–40 lakhs (complete course). EMI/loan options available.
Are these universities recognized by NMC / WHO?
Yes, all our partnered universities are listed in NMC (formerly MCI) & WHO directories, eligible for FMGE/NEXT.
